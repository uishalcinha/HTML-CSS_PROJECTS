About

Hi! My name is Roushan Sinha.
I’m a graphic designer based in Vancouver. I design high-concept digital experiences for businesses, and bring a unique perspective to design through my background in film and video. I have had the privelage to work with clients such as Fairmont, BC Liquor, Vancouver is Awesome, and more. Before I was a designer, I worked in music video production as a resident videographer and content creator. I’ve spent countless hours working with music video directors to help bring their stories to life, from designing their pitch decks, to filming on set, to editing their music videos. Today, I bring my love of storytelling to design, and take influence in film, art, music and design. I believe in using the power of narrative to create emotional connections between business and consumer, and bring with me a multitude of technical skills that can elevate your projects above the rest.
